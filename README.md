## 🚀 Interactive 3D Barrier Playground

Open [`3D_barrier_generation_playground.html`](./3D_barrier_generation_playground.html) in any browser to explore the barrier construction interactively.

**Features:**
- 4 construction stages: terrain sketch → step approximation → apex shifting → 3D barrier surface
- Live readout of lateral & vertical clearances
- Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom
- Keyboard shortcuts (`1–4` stages, `WASD/QE` lander, `R` reset, `?` help)

## 🎬 Simulation Animation

![Soft Landing Trajectories Animation](isro_proj_animation.gif)

The animation shows three spacecraft landing trajectories (Cases 1–3) in the X-Z and Y-Z planes, each starting from different initial positions and velocities under the proposed fuel-efficient guidance law.
